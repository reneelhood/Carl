# Quiz 3: 90-Day Business Goal Planner
## For Business Coaches

**Purpose:** High-value lead magnet. Client fills it out = they commit to goals before they even hire a coach.

**Structure:**
**Section 1: Vision (Week 1-2)**
- Where do you want your business to be in 90 days?
- Revenue goal: $_____
- Team size: _____
- Personal goals (vacation, family time): _____

**Section 2: Obstacles (Week 3-4)**
- What's stopping you NOW? (check all that apply)
  - ☐ Not enough leads
  - ☐ Can't close deals
  - ☐ Team issues
  - ☐ Burnout
  - ☐ Need strategy

**Section 3: The Plan (Week 5-8)**
- Month 1 focus: _____
- Month 2 focus: _____
- Month 3 focus: _____

**Section 4: Accountability (Week 9-12)**
- Who's holding you accountable?
- What happens if you miss goals?

**Outcomes:**
- Generates a custom 90-day PDF roadmap
- Coach gets full visibility into client's mindset before the sales call

**Lead Capture:** Full name + email + phone (for appointment booking)
