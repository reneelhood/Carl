# GPT Demo 1: Dental Office Assistant
## For Local Dentists

**Niche:** Dental/Medical

**What it does:**
- Answers FAQs about services, pricing, insurance
- Schedules appointment inquiries
- Provides after-care instructions
- Handles "Do you take my insurance?" type questions

**Training Data Included:**
- Services list (cleaning, whitening, cavities, implants)
- Pricing ranges
- Accepted insurance providers
- Office hours & location
- Common dental FAQs

**Client Pain Point:**
Front desk spends 20+ hours/week answering the same questions. This bot handles 80% of inquiries instantly.
