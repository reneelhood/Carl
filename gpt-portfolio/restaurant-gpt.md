# GPT Demo 3: Restaurant Concierge
## For Local Restaurants

**Niche:** Food & Hospitality

**What it does:**
- Answers menu questions (allergens, dietary needs)
- Handles reservation inquiries
- Takes pickup/delivery orders
- Provides hours, directions, parking info
- Handles event/private party inquiries

**Training Data Included:**
- Full menu with prices, allergens, dietary tags
- Hours of operation
- Reservation policy
- Private event packages
- Location & parking info

**Client Pain Point:**
Staff gets overwhelmed on busy shifts. This bot handles 50+ inquiries per day — questions, reservations, orders — freeing up staff to focus on dine-in guests.
