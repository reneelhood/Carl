# Quiz 2: Does Your Business Need a Coach?
## For Business Coaches

**Purpose:** Pre-qualify leads — if they answer "yes" to most, they're primed for a sales conversation.

**Questions:**
1. Is your revenue where you want it to be?
   - Yes / No / Kind of

2. Do you feel like you're working IN the business instead of ON it?
   - Yes / No / Sometimes

3. Do you have clear systems for marketing, sales, and delivery?
   - Full systems / Some / None

4. Are you ready to invest in your own growth (time & money)?
   - Yes, committed / Maybe / Not yet

5. Do you have 2+ hours/week to dedicate to coaching?
   - Yes / No / I'd make time

**Scoring:**
- 4-5 "No" or "Not yet" answers = Hot Lead
- 2-3 = Warm
- 0-1 = Not ready yet

**Follow-up:**
- Hot: "Here's your custom action plan" → Book call
- Warm: "Here's a free resource" → Nurture sequence
- Not ready: "Here's a mini-course" → Stay in touch

---

**Lead Capture:** Name + Email for results
