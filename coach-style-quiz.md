# Quiz 1: Find Your Coaching Style
## For Business Coaches

**Purpose:** Help potential clients discover what type of coaching they need — positions the coach as the solution.

**Questions:**
1. What's your biggest business challenge right now?
   - A) Growing my team
   - B) Scaling revenue
   - C) Work-life balance
   - D) Strategic clarity

2. How do you prefer to receive coaching?
   - A) 1-on-1 calls
   - B) Group programs
   - C) Self-paced courses
   - D) Mix of everything

3. What's your timeline?
   - A) Quick wins (30 days)
   - B) Long-term transformation (6mo+)
   - C) Just exploring

4. What's your investment range?
   - A) Under $500
   - B) $500-2k
   - C) $2k-10k
   - D) Whatever it takes

**Outcomes:**
- Strategic Coach (mostly A's)
- Growth Scaler (mostly B's)
- Balance Builder (mostly C's)
- Explorer (mostly D's)
